# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want this project space to be respectful, practical, and inclusive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on factual, actionable collaboration.
- Assume good intent and keep discussions professional.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abusive language.
- Doxxing, threats, or discriminatory behavior.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
